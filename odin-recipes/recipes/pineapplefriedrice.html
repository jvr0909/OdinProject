Pineapple Fried Rice

Description

Pineapple fried rice is a delicious and vibrant dish that combines the sweetness of pineapple with savory flavors and textures from the vegetables and cashews. It's a versatile recipe, and you can customize it by adding cooked shrimp, chicken, or tofu for added protein. This tropical twist on traditional fried rice is perfect for gatherings, potlucks, or simply as a flavorful meal on its own. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked jasmine rice
  • 1 medium-sized pineapple
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1/2 cup cashews, roughly chopped
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cut the pineapple in half lengthwise and scoop out the flesh, leaving the shells intact. Dice the pineapple flesh into small chunks and set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and saut√© until they become translucent and fragrant.
  3. Add the diced bell pepper, carrot, and frozen peas to the skillet. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables become tender-crisp.
  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the cooked jasmine rice to the other side. Break up any clumps and stir-fry the rice for a couple of minutes, allowing it to heat through.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce and curry powder until well combined. Pour the mixture over the rice and vegetables, stirring well to evenly distribute the flavors.
  6. Add the diced pineapple, cashews, and chopped green onions to the skillet. Stir-fry for another minute or two, ensuring all the ingredients are well combined and heated through.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remember that the soy sauce already contains salt, so adjust accordingly.
  8. Carefully spoon the pineapple fried rice mixture back into the pineapple shells. Garnish with additional chopped green onions or cashews if desired.
  9. Serve the pineapple fried rice while it's still hot and enjoy!
